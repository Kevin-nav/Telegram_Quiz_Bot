# Academic Structure: Faculties, Programmes, and Courses

This document outlines the academic hierarchy, including faculties, their respective programmes, and the courses offered (currently populated for Level 100).

## Hierarchy Structure

The system's data is structured hierarchically as follows:
- **Level** (e.g., Level 100)
- **Faculty**
  - **Program** (Belongs to a Faculty)
    - **Course** (Associated with a specific Level and one or multiple Programs)
